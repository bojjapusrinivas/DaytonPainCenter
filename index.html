Welcome to Dayton Pain Center

  • Dayton Pain Center (DPC) offers a comprehensive range of services for patients with acute or chronic pain. Through the combined expertise of a multidisciplinary team of pain specialists, DPC offers the most advanced treatment options currently available, in a supportive, compassionate environment.
  • Pain is a complex medical problem that can have profound effects on your physical and mental well-being. Our goal is to help you decrease your level of pain and suffering, to return you to your maximum level of functioning and independence, and to help you restore your quality of life. The DPC provides care for hospitalized and ambulatory patients with acute, chronic and cancer pain problems.
  • Our services cover a vast geographic area in and around the greater Dayton area, encompassing an 80 mile radius along the interstates 75 and 40. We are the only facility with multiple service locations and offer the largest number of providers and highly qualified board certified physicians specializing in Pain, Addiction, Neurosurgery, Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation, Electromyography, Spinal Cord injuries, Critical Care, Hospice Palliative Care. Chiropractic Care, Psychotherapists and Counselling.
